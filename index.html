Frida Kahlo's Facts:

  • Born Magdalena Carmen Frida Kahlo y Calderon on July 6,1907
  • Mexican artist who painted portraits and amazing self-portraits
  • Went to National Preparatory School focusing on natural sciences with goals of being a doctor. An accident in 1925 put an end to these dreams.
